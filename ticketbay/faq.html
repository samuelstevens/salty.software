Frequently Asked Questions


Do I have to pay any money to use TicketBay?

No! TicketBay is 100% free of any and all fees. Just sign up with your .edu email account and start advertising and finding tickets!

Do I pay or recieve money through TicketBay?

No. TicketBay functions as a way to connect ticket buyers and sellers. The app does not handle any transactions or money processing. The exchange of goods, meet-up location, and other transaction details are determined by you and the other buyer/seller once you reach out to each other through our app.

Is TicketBay available for my college or university?

Unfortunately, if your school is not THE Ohio State University, then no. However, we do plan to roll out support for other schools in the very near future.
