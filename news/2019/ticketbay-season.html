TicketBay's Season Review

Feb 5th, 2019

We've been absolutely astounded by the success of TicketBay in its first season, and we owe it all to the users. We have lots of plans for the coming months and seasons, and we're excited to share them with you!


The four of us were excited to release an app for our fellow students, and had very low expectations for our success. We thought that if we could help at least one student sell a football ticket, then we would have learned an incredible amount and helped a Buckeye out. Win-win for everyone involved.


In an incredible turn of events, we had waaay more than 1 person sell a ticket. By the end of the season, we had 2700 users, who listed nearly 1700 tickets. Throughout the season, 44 THOUSAND dollars worth of tickets was transferred through the app between students.


Clearly, there's a large demand for a service like TicketBay on campus. We were amazed by both the amount of people who used the app as well as the positive feedback we received. After such a successful season, we're certainly going to work more on the app and update for the next football season.


We're currently thinking about what improvements we can make to the app, and would welcome any feedback from users! Feel free to email us at support@salty.software.
